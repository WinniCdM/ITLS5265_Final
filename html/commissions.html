Commissions

Here you can submit a request for a commission. This can be anywhere from a simple sketch to a full scale scene painting. I currently only offer commissions in the digital medium. Please fill out the below form completely, and submit. I will get back to you as soon as I can.

My prices average at about $30 per hour.

Disclosure:

The artist, henceforth known as "I", will provide a price upon initial contact. Some light negotiating is allowed, however, I hold the right to set the final price. I hold the right to accept or deny any commission made. Half payment is required after a price is agreed upon and before I begin the process. The rest is required after a rough sketch is approved.

I am willing to draw just about anything, but know I am best with humanoids, dragon-esk creatures, and just about any sort of winged creature. I will not do any form of nudity or sexual content, at all.

Please allow 2 to 4 weeks for completion based on complexity. I draw and paint as a hobby and do not do this full time, so please be patient with me. I will be as quick as I can.

I have read and agree to the above disclosure.
